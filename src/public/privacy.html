Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the Ad Manager Earnings Tracker app (“the App”).

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we access your authorized Google Ad Manager data to generate reports. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data or Ad Manager data on our servers. All data is retrieved in real-time and used only for your session.

Google APIs

The App uses the Google OAuth API to request access to your Ad Manager account. This access is strictly read-only. Your credentials and tokens are not stored.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate analytics reports from your Ad Manager account.
  • To display report data during your session.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties.

Security

All communication with Google APIs is secured via HTTPS. No tokens or reports are persisted or shared.

Contact

If you have questions, email us at holymarkdev@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
